 Background story

My name is Alex, and I am a computer science graduate, grown in the dot-com bubble with a contagious curiosity for photography and tech. I studied Computer Science at the Babes-Bolyai University in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, for the last 3 years and I plan on continuing with a Master's Degree on Artificial Intelligence.

Growing up, I always had such a passion for technology, whether that be watching marvelous pieces of engineering, writing algorithms to solve my class assignments, or even just taking pictures and editing them. Being able to express myself in pictures is the driving force that allowed me to evolve and get involved in adjacent activities. For most of the pictures I do sweat (like a lot) and it is exhausing, yet it is one of those activities asking for creative decisions and it really helps to go through the process, from time to time.
